Creating Access to Finance for Farmers

We enable individuals and institutions to fund the capital requirement of the farmers. GrowAgri bundles finance with agriculture inputs, advisory services, insurance and market access for the farmers. We use an agriculturally relevant and data-driven model to assess risk and return to develop funding schemes that fit the needs of smallholder farmers and the farm funders.

Smart Supply Chain

supply chain involves sourcing funds for farmers, providing agricultural support, coordinating cultivation, harvest collection, quality control, packaging, distribution, and market sales. To implement an efficient agriculture funding system, establish partnerships with financial institutions, government agencies, and agricultural organizations to secure funding. Utilize digital platforms for transparent fund allocation and provide farmers with access to resources and expertise. Through effective coordination and technology integration, optimize operations to ensure sustainable farming practices, quality produce, and market success, fostering economic growth in agriculture.
